About

Ben, an accomplished engineer and software developer, possesses a deep fascination for the intricacies found within geospatial data. His journey into the realm of artistry began when he became captivated by the nuanced details and mesmerizing patterns inherent in landscapes. Driven by his passion, he embarked on a creative endeavor, transforming his fascination into tangible pieces of art. Inspired by a desire to bring the beauty of geographical features into the lives of others, Ben began crafting exquisite wall art pieces using his unique approach. In his artistic process, Ben meticulously harnesses various sources of geospatial data, delving into realms such as open geospatial data, lidar technology, radar imaging, photogrammetry techniques, and satellite data. With an expert eye, he carefully curates this diverse array of data, weaving them into intricate 3D models that encapsulate the essence of the landscapes he explores. Each model is a testament to his meticulous research and dedication, capturing the essence of natural wonders and human-made marvels alike. Through his mastery of technology and artistry, Ben then brings these meticulously crafted 3D models to life, employing cutting-edge 3D printing techniques. The result of his passion and expertise is a collection of awe-inspiring artworks that not only showcase the beauty of the Earth's topography but also reflect Ben's unwavering commitment to precision and creativity. Each piece invites viewers to embark on a visual journey, appreciating the fusion of scientific data and artistic expression, making Ben's creations not just art, but a testament to the seamless blend of technology and creativity.
